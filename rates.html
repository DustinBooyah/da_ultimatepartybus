Reservation Policy
  • 5 hour minimal is required. 8 hours or more recieve a discount!
  • Book Online for a 5% discount!
  • A 50% NON-REFUNDABLE deposit will be charged upon reservation.
  • Balance will be charged 72 hours prior to reserved pickup date.
  • An 8% fuel surcharge will be added to the reservation total.
  • Gratuity is NOT included. Please tip your chauffeur 15-20%.
  • Reservations for 6 or more hours, please request a quote or call for a discounted rate!
  • Cancellations must be made in writing and faxed/mailed no less than 7 days prior to reserved pickup date in order to receive a 50% refund. NO REFUND is available if cancellations are made less than 7 days prior to pickup date.
  • Rescheduling is available at no additional charge and must be made prior to scheduled pickup date. Service may be rescheduled for up to 30 days after original pickup date.
